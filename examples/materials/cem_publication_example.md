# Cost-Effectiveness Publication Excerpt

The publication reports that a partitioned-survival model was considered, but a Markov model was selected due to data availability and transparency for validation.

Key assumption: treatment effect waning begins after year 3 in base case.

Validation notes: internal validity checks compared model-predicted survival with trial Kaplan-Meier observations. External validity assessed against registry benchmarks.

Open issue: subgroup-specific health-state utilities were unavailable and borrowed from a related population.
